      ℹ️ Grow a Garden 2 Predictor Info

      Important details about predictability & logic rules

      • • Weather forecasts may vary by server and are approximately 85% accurate.
      • • Gear predictions may be inaccurate, especially for the Super Watering Can.
      • • Predictions may break during updates or when some servers are running a newer version than others without a full restart.
      • • If all stock predictions show no restock or weather data is missing, the predictor may need to be updated to the latest data. You can request a predictor update by visiting GitHub.

      • Frequently Asked Questions

      • What is the Grow a Garden 2 Predictor website?

        The Grow a Garden 2 Predictor is a live stock tracker and weather predictor tool for the popular Roblox game Grow a Garden 2. It tracks exactly when seeds, gear, and crates will restock in the shop.

      • Where can I find Grow a Garden 2 codes?

        Grow a Garden 2 codes are periodically released by developers. While this predictor website tracks stock and weather parameters, check our detailed guide or the official Grow a Garden 2 Discord server for active codes.

      • How does the weather system cycle in Grow a Garden 2?

        The weather phases (Day, Sunset, Moon, Bloodmoon, Goldmoon, Rainbow Moon, Mega Moon) shift on regular cycle times. Use our Weather predictor to view exact local start times and remaining duration for upcoming weather events.

      • What are the rarest pets in Grow a Garden 2?

        Pets range from Common to Divine rarities, including special variants like Big, Mega, and Rainbow. Rarity values and pet multipliers help players gain faster garden growth.
